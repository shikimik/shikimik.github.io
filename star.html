The most outstanding information systems

These are the main systems that exist:

Information systems in marketing and communication(MC):

Its main objective is the sale and promotion of articles. The creation of information and data so that these types of articles can be made known and are of interest to the public.



The administrative information system (MIS):

It is one of the most important, and is based on providing the most outstanding and relevant information that the company needs for decision making, and data assessment to carry out a subsequent action plan. based on this collection of documents.



The transaction processing system (TPS):

In this case, the data included here refers to the commercial activities of the company.



The executive support system (EIS):

Specially designed to provide relevant information and data for making specific strategic decisions.



The group decision-making system (GDSS):

The information is intended to be shared between the components of the group so that work tasks and cooperation among all are facilitated.



The information system for experts (EDSS):

In this case, what we are trying to achieve is to share data of interest between certain segments and specialists to assess that information intended for them.
